# Architecture

## What AFT Deploys

AFT provisions three frameworks in Management:

- **Request** — EventBridge, Lambda, DynamoDB
- **Provisioning** — Step Functions, Service Catalog
- **Customisation** — CodePipeline, CodeBuild, S3
